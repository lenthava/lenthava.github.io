    About

    I am presently an undergraduate student of Computer Science at the University of Basel, enrolled in the Department of Mathematics and Computer Science. On track to graduate in the next 2-4 semesters, I initially completed my first 4 semesters at ETH Zurich. Beyond my academic pursuits, I seized the opportunity in 2021 to take on a minor managerial role at my father's small tiling business, playing a pivotal role in expanding contracts and cultivating strong business relationships. Furthermore, I have concurrently held a part-time position at Sprachzentrum Winterthur, where I serve as a proctor for German SDS exams.


    I'm passionate about promoting 'smart contracts' with blockchain technology for transparent online transactions, aiming to eliminate intermediaries and explore blockchain's broader applications beyond finance.

    I'm fascinated by business and startups, drawing inspiration from Masayoshi Son of Softbank and their significant investments globally. Son's journey, from overcoming adversity to building Softbank into a telecommunications giant with successful investments, continues to inspire me. I have a deep fascination with Japan, especially its rich culture, modern cities, cuisine, and high-speed trains, particularly during the vibrant "bubble era" in the 90s.

    I'm fascinated by Singapore for its competent leadership, futuristic cityscape, and vibrant cultural diversity, boasting the world's busiest port and an impressive airport. After my visit in February 2023, I see it as the leading country in Asia.

    Regarding India, I recognize its potential as a promising investment hub, admiring the population's drive, adaptability, and commitment to education. The market's appeal for future innovations lies in the efficient use of resources, especially among the growing middle class, embracing globalization.

    Cycling is a beloved pastime of mine, offering a space for mindfulness and idea generation. I enjoy exploring Möhlin and Rheinfelden in Switzerland, occasionally crossing the border to cycle in Rheinfelden, Germany, accompanied by soothing music that enhances the experience.

    Moreover, I have a profound fondness for Japanese anime, particularly those set in futuristic or fantastical worlds, such as "Neon Genesis Evangelion", "Cowboy Bebop", and the "Ghost in the Shell” series, including “Stand Alone Complex," which offer compelling narratives and visionary storytelling despite being created in the past.

    If my interests resonate with you and you wish to talk to me (about anything!), please don't hesitate to reach out. I would be delighted to connect with you.
